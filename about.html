Our Mission

“At MITHub, our mission is to simplify student life at MIT by offering a one-stop platform for affordable and tailored products and services.”

“MITHub was created by a group of passionate MIT students who wanted to address the everyday challenges faced by their peers. From setting up dorm rooms to finding affordable resources, we realized the need for a community-driven marketplace tailored to our unique needs.”

Student Discounts: Affordable pricing to help manage tight budgets.
Personalized Recommendations: AI-driven suggestions based on your preferences.
Community-Focused: A marketplace designed by and for MIT students.
